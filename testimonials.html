FAQ's

How do Mixologiq machines work?

Plug in, load your bottles, select a recipe, and serve. Every cocktail is poured with precision and consistency in under 30 seconds.

What‚Äôs the difference between Mixo 8 and Mixo 20?

Mixo 8 is compact with 8 refrigerated bottles, designed for focused menus and fast service. Mixo 20 is our flagship with 20 bottles, built for variety and large-scale venues. Both share the same precision engineering, refrigeration, and back office system.

What types of cocktails can I make?

From classics to modern favorites, Mixologiq supports over 300 recipes. Each machine comes preloaded with recommended cocktails to get you started.

What types of cocktails can I make?

From classics to modern favorites, Mixologiq supports over 300 recipes. Each machine comes preloaded with recommended cocktails to get you started.

Do the machines require special installation?

No. Both models are stand-alone, require no water inlet, and only need a standard power connection.

Are the bottles refrigerated?

Yes. All bottles in every Mixologiq machine are fully refrigerated to keep ingredients fresh and cocktails consistent.

Can staff with no cocktail experience use it?

Yes. Our machines are designed for simplicity. Any team member can serve perfect cocktails after just a few minutes of training.

How much maintenance is required?

Mixologiq machines are built for easy upkeep with semi-automatic cleaning and accessible parts. We recommend twice yearly preventative servicing for best performance. Distributors provide full support and training.

Is there support after purchase?

Absolutely. Mixologiq provides training, resources, and after-sales support through our distributor network worldwide.
